India

Festivals

Diwali

Diwali, Festival of lights, is India's most celebrated festivals.

Holi

Holi,the Festival of colors, is known for its vibrant celebrations.

Eid

Eid is significant Muslim festival marked by prayers and feasting.

Places to Visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO world heritage site

Jaipur

Jaipur, often referrred to as the Pink City, is known for historic sites, including:

  • Amer fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace

Kerala

Kerala is famous serene backwater and lush green landscapes.

Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is creamy and flavorful Indian dish.

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and aromatic rice dish with various regional variations. Notable ingredients include:

  1. Basmati rice
  2. Chicken or Lamb(vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  3. Spices (cumin, coriander and garam masala)
  4. Yogurt and saffron for flacor and color

Dosa

Dosa is South Indian crispy pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter.
