Mumbai City Cuisine

Mumbai is culinary paradise with a diverse range of flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and food experiences in the city:

Pav Bhaji

A popular street food dish consisting of a spicy vegetavle curry (bhaji) served with soft bread rolls (pav).

Bhel Puri

A delighful combination of puffed rice, sev, chutneys, and various ingredients, creating a tasty snack.
