History of Mumbai City

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, has a rich history that dates back centuries. Here are key milestones that shaped the city.

Ancient Period

Mumbai has evidence of human habitation dating back to the Stone Age. The city was a collection of islands inhabited by the kol fishing community.

Independence and Post-Independence

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, played a pivotal role in India's independence movement and continued to evolve into the country's financial, commercial, and cultural capital after gaining freedom in 1947.

Economic Hub

Mumbai, often referred to as the financial capital of India, is the country's largest economic center and a major contributor to national growth. It serves as a global financial hub, a thriving commercial hub, and a gateway to international trade.
