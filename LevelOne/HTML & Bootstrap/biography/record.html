Records

Kohli became the fastest player to score 27,000 runs in international cricket. He surpassed Sachin Tendulkar's record, achieving the feat in 594 innings compared to Tendulkar's 623, during the second Test against Bangladesh at Green Park Stadium. Kohli now joins Ricky Ponting and Kumar Sangakkara as the only players to have crossed this milestone in international cricket.

Kohli possesses technical abilities and a dynamic playing demeanour.
